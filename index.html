Common Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting.

Do you serve authentic Goan food?

Yes, we specialize in authentic Goan home-style cooking. Our family recipes for Fish Curry, Recheado Masala, and Vindaloo are prepared fresh daily.

Where are you located in South Goa?

We are located in Karashirmoll, Agonda. We are a short distance from the main Agonda beach area, offering a peaceful family atmosphere.

Is your restaurant family-friendly?

Absolutely. Soul Curry is a Family Bar & Restaurant with spacious seating, a safe environment, and a free pool table for entertainment.

Do you serve fresh seafood?

We pride ourselves on serving only the freshest catch of the day from local fishermen. From Kingfish to Prawns, our seafood is never frozen.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes! While we are famous for seafood, our Authentic Goan Veg Thali and vegetable curries are loved by locals and tourists alike.

Do you accept group bookings?

We love hosting big families and groups! We have plenty of space, a pool table, and can arrange special buffet meals for parties on request.
