The Soul of Goa on a Plate

Soul Curry isn't just a restaurant; it’s a tribute to the authentic culinary heritage of Agonda. Born from a desire to share Leston's family recipes with the world, our kitchen is a place where tradition meets passion.

Every dish tells a story—from our Fresh Kingfish caught by local fishermen at dawn, to our signature Recheado Masala ground by hand using age-old techniques. Whether you're here for a competitive game of Snooker (Free for guests!) or to unwind with a cold beer after a day at the beach, we promise you the warmest welcome in South Goa.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting.

What is the best authentic Goan food restaurant in Agonda?

Yes, we specialize in authentic Goan home-style cooking. Our family recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring every curry has that real Goan soul.

Where to eat in Agonda South Goa — how do I find Soul Curry?

We are centrally located in Agonda, South Goa, just a few minutes' walk from the beach. You can find us easily on Google Maps or just ask any local for "Soul Curry"!

Is Soul Curry Agonda good for families visiting South Goa?

Absolutely! We are a family-run restaurant and take pride in being one of the most welcoming spots for families in Agonda. We have a relaxed atmosphere and a free pool table for kids and adults alike.

Where can I get the freshest seafood in Agonda Goa?

Right here! We source our seafood daily from local fishermen. Our "Catch of the Day" depends on what was caught that morning, ensuring maximum freshness.

Does Soul Curry Agonda have vegetarian Goan food options?

Yes! While we are famous for seafood, our Authentic Goan Veg Thali and various veg curries are very popular with our vegetarian guests.

Can I make a group booking at Soul Curry Agonda?

Yes, we welcome large groups! Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or just a large gang of friends, please message us on WhatsApp in advance so we can reserve the perfect spot for you.
